# Phase 1: Idea & Scoping

## Project Name

**SustainScore**

## App Concept

SustainScore is a mobile application that helps users monitor and improve their daily sustainability habits. The app allows users to log simple resource-related activities, such as lighting usage, washing machine usage, computer or television usage, heating or cooling behavior.

Based on the logged activities, the application calculates a sustainability score that helps users understand how their daily choices affect their resource consumption. The app also compares the user's actual behavior with a recommended sustainable behavior profile, making it easier to see how small changes could improve their score.

## Target Users

The target users are students and household users who want a simple way to understand and improve their daily resource consumption habits.

The application is intended for users who may not have smart-home devices or advanced energy-monitoring tools, but still want to become more aware of how their daily actions affect sustainability.

## Value Proposition

SustainScore makes sustainability easier to understand by transforming everyday actions into a clear score, visual comparison, and improvement suggestions.

Instead of showing only raw consumption data, the app helps users identify patterns in their behavior, understand which activities have the highest impact, and discover practical ways to improve. This gives users a simple and motivating way to follow their progress over time.

## Key Features

### 1. Activity Logging

Users can log predefined daily activities related to resource consumption, such as the number of lights in use, appliance usage duration, or heating and cooling behavior.

### 2. Sustainability Score

The application calculates a sustainability score based on the user's logged activities. This score helps users understand whether their behavior is becoming more or less sustainable.

### 3. Visual Comparison

The app displays the user's actual behavior alongside a recommended sustainable profile using charts. This helps users see how their choices compare with a better scenario.

### 4. Daily and Weekly Summaries

The app provides summary views showing the user's average score, high-consumption periods, and progress over time.

### 5. AI-Generated Feedback

The application provides short, actionable feedback generated from the user's activity data. The feedback suggests realistic ways to reduce unnecessary consumption and improve sustainability habits.
